# Adidas Sales Performance Dashboard (2020-2021)

## Project Description:
This project was designed as a comprehensive Business Intelligence solution for Adidas, aiming to provide clear and accurate insights into retail sales performance.
The dashboard compares two consecutive years (2020 and 2021) to transform static sales numbers into actionable insights that tell a story of growth, trends, and seasonal demand.

## üìà Analysis Structure
The analysis is structured into four main sections:

### 1. Executive Sales Summary:
This section presents the overall performance through key metrics, including:
* **Total Sales:** $190M
* **Units Sold:** 2M
* **Operating Profit:** $47.2M
It also displays sales trends over 24 months, helping identify periods of significant growth.

### 2. Smart Geographical Analysis:
This section focuses on regional and market-level performance, highlighting top-performing cities such as New York, and providing insights into areas driving annual growth.

### 3. Product Analysis:
This section dives into individual product performance, showing monthly sales trends over the two years. This helps management identify products with increasing or decreasing demand.

### 4. Pricing and Consumer Behavior:
Functioning as a financial control panel, this section provides insights into purchasing patterns, comparing weekday sales ($88.3M) versus weekend sales ($31.8M), and measuring the impact of major events such as Black Friday, which generated over $313K alone.

## üíº Business Value & Impact:
* **Identify Annual Growth Trends:** Year-over-year comparison highlights growth and decline periods, enabling accurate forecasting for upcoming seasons.
* **Optimize Marketing Decisions:** Temporal analysis helps marketing teams determine the best timing for campaigns and promotions.
* **Smart Inventory Management:** Understanding sales velocity per product allows more accurate stock planning and prevents stockouts of high-demand items.
* **Enhance Omni-Channel Sales Performance:** Comparing in-store, online, and outlet sales helps identify the most profitable channels and guides strategic investments.
